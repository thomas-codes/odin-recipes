Lambchops Recipe

Description

Lamb Chops have a flavorful garlic and herb crust with a juicy and tender center. These holiday-worthy lamb chops are simple and excellent and wait until you try the easy 2-ingredient pan sauce!

Ingredients

  • Lamb rib chops
  • Garlic cloves
  • Parsley
  • Tabsco sauce
  • Seasonings
  • Chicken Stock
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Thoroughly pat lamb chops dry with paper towels, wiping away any possible bone fragments or shards. Slice between ribs to separate chops, cutting them into even portions (3/4″ to 1″ thick.
  2. In a measuring cup, stir together garlic, olive oil, parsley, Tabasco, salt, pepper and thyme
  3. Place chops in a non-reactive casserole dish and pour marinade over the chops. Rub the marinade all over the chops, then cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight.
  4. Remove chops from the refrigerator 30 minutes before cooking. Heat a large heavy pan over high heat. Add 1 Tbsp oil to the hot pan. Add chops and sear for 2-4 minutes per side, depending on the thickness of the chops and desired doneness (see chart below). Sautee in 2 batches if needed.
  5. Transfer to a platter, tent with foil and rest the lamb chops for 5 minutes while making the pan sauce
  6. Leave 1 to 2 Tbsp oil in the pan, being careful not to remove the drippings and flavorings. Add 1/2 cup stock and simmer 2 min. Turn off the heat then swirl in butter. Spoon sauce over lamb chops and garnish with chopped parsley if desired.
